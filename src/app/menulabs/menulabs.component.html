TANSAM is a government initiative supporting educational institutions, industries, MSMEs, start-ups, and OEMs. It aims to educate students and professionals on Industry 4.0 technologies, providing hands-on experience with industrial hardware and software.

Using the aforementioned seven Centers, TANSAM provides solutions to all industries across India. TANSAM is providing services in the aforementioned seven centers to all Start -up’s, MSME’s, SME’s, Industries, and OEM’s. Also, we are assisting students, employees, start – up’s, MSME’s in learning the skills necessary in their industry and provide them with practical experience with all industrial hardware and software. The aforementioned seven areas will assist in turning an idea into a functioning product.

Services We Provide

  • Industry solutions using our 7 specialized centers
  • Practical skill development aligned with industrial needs
  • Access to modern software and hardware
  • Support in turning ideas into functional products
  • Hands-on training and real-world use cases
